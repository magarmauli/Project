How It's Made Starbucks' Coffee, Milk, Coffee Machines

Starbucks uses a multi-stage process to make its coffee and milk-based drinks.

From sourcing high-quality coffee beans to mastering the art of steaming milk,

each step is carefully managed to ensure a consistent, high-quality taste. Starbucks'

coffee production involves everything from selecting and harvesting beans to roasting,

and brewing. They also use a range of coffee machines, including Clover Vertica,

and train baristas on how to make milk drinks like lattes with precision.
